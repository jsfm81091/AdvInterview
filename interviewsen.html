How to Conduct a
Successful Interview

When facing a job interview, job-seeking professionals often have many questions about how to handle the situation and come out successfully from the selection process.

Below, we provide you with some tips on how you should showcase your skills, competencies, and knowledge during the interview to make it successful.


Preparation


Preparation is the first step to conducting a successful interview. The more prepared you are, the more confident you will feel.


Be sure to know:


  • The exact time and location of the interview.
  • Specific information about the company.
  • Prepare some questions for your interviewer.

What to do:


  • Arrive on time or a few minutes early.
  • Shake hands firmly.
  • Try to be as charismatic as possible.
  • Maintain eye contact with your interviewer.
  • Try to convey to your interviewer in a concise, specific, and sincere manner what your strengths are.
